This Retainer Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of March 1, 2024 by and between Pinnacle Legal Group ("Firm") and the estate of Reginald P. Bigglesworth, deceased, acting through its executor Fuzzy Paws Inc. ("Client"), collectively the "Parties."
Engagement Details
Matter TypeEstate Litigation
Matter DescriptionBigglesworth Estate v. Fuzzy Paws Inc.
Hourly Rate$650.00 / hour
Retainer Amount$25,000.00
Term12 months from effective date
Scope of Representation
The Firm agrees to represent the Client in all matters related to the probate and distribution of the Bigglesworth Estate, including but not limited to: contesting the validity of the third codicil (the "cat inheritance clause"), negotiating with Fuzzy Paws Inc. regarding the disputed Cayman Islands property, and defending against cross-claims filed by Dr. Von Stuffington regarding unpaid expert witness fees.
