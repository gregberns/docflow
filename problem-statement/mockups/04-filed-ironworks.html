Change Description
Client has requested conversion of the planned infinity pool (Drawing A-14, Rev 3) to a decorative moat with pedestrian bridge. Scope includes: demolition of existing pool excavation and rebar cage, re-grading for moat channel (approximately 180 linear feet, 8ft wide, 4ft depth), installation of alligator-grade waterproofing membrane, recirculating pump system, decorative stone lining, and a 12ft arched pedestrian bridge with wrought-iron railings.

Note: Client has been advised that alligator-grade waterproofing is typically specified for zoo applications. Client insists. Subcontractor (Exotic Aquatic Enclosures LLC) has confirmed availability.
